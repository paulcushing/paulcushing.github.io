I'm a big fan of Wordle. I came in late since I don't always keep up with the trends, but after I heard an interview with Josh Wardle on the Syntax.fm podcast, I checked it out... and I LOVE word games.

We all know there are better ways to cheat of course. I built this tool purely as a challenge. I was curious how I would go about programmatically finding the words that fit the criteria I had to work with.

So here you are. A Wordle helper if you will. Best of luck!

 

Also, if you want to see what the code looks like or suggest some features or fixes, you'll find it all at https://paulcushing.github.io/.
